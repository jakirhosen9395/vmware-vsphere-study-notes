# Books and Blogs

Recommended reading materials and blogs for VMware vSphere learning.

## Books

### Beginner Level
- **"Mastering VMware vSphere"** - Essential guide for beginners
- **"VMware vSphere For Dummies"** - Easy introduction to vSphere

### Intermediate Level
- **"vSphere 7.0 Clustering Deep Dive"** - In-depth coverage of HA, DRS
- **"VMware vSphere Performance"** - Performance tuning guide

### Advanced Level
- **"VMware vSphere Design"** - Best practices for vSphere design
- **"VMware NSX Cookbook"** - Advanced networking with NSX
