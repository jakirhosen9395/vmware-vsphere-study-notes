# Books and Blogs

Curated list of recommended books, blogs, and online resources for VMware vSphere learning.

## Recommended Books

### vSphere Fundamentals
- **Mastering VMware vSphere** by Nick Marshall, Mike Brown
  - Comprehensive guide covering all aspects of vSphere
  - Great for both beginners and experienced administrators
  
- **VMware vSphere 8.0 Administration Instant Reference** by Christopher Wahl, Steve Pantol
  - Quick reference guide for daily admin tasks
  - Covers common scenarios and solutions

- **VMware vSphere For Dummies** by Daniel Mitchell
  - Beginner-friendly introduction to vSphere
  - Step-by-step approach to key concepts

### Advanced Topics
- **VMware vSphere Performance: Designing CPU, Memory, Storage, and Networking for Performance-Intensive Workloads** by Matt Liebowitz, Christopher Kusek, Rynardt Spies
  - Deep dive into performance optimization
  - Essential for production environments

- **VMware vSphere Design** by Forbes Guthrie, Scott Lowe, Kendrick Coleman
  - Best practices for vSphere design
  - Real-world deployment scenarios

### Automation and Scripting
- **VMware vSphere PowerCLI Reference: Automating vSphere Administration** by Luc Dekens, Alan Renouf, Glenn Sizemore
  - Comprehensive PowerCLI guide
  - Practical automation examples
